About Hotel Royal Bengal

Hotel Royal Bengal promises flavoursome International and Indian scullery along with a pleasant ambience at the Sonajhuri Restaurant. Rejuvenate and relax in the on-site lavish garden and the expansive outdoor pool.Business travellers can make the most of meetings and event halls by hosting business affairs.

The hotel has 37 well-equipped air-conditioned rooms filled with natural light during daytime for lasting luxury and peace of mind for the travellers. The Hotel Royal Bengal features an AC Deluxe room, AC Executive room, AC Superior triple bedroom and the Deluxe triple bedrooms. Indulge in the tranquil rooms with all necessary amenities such as a telephone, locker in the room, free Wi-Fi, tea/coffee maker, a balcony and attached bathroom with hot and cold running water. Each room varies from others when it comes to the interiors, the presence of a balcony and overall square feet area of the room. All rooms have a beautiful garden view and 24-hour room service. Travellers selected the property because of its rooms.
