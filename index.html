Great Location

Hotel Royal Bengal, a boutique hotel offering leisure, luxury & peace of mind. Nestled in the serene and green environs , the cultural hot spot of Bengal. Spacious property overlooking the indoor stadium & greenery, at the heart of Kolkata and offers guests the chance to get away from the noise and activity of city life, while still having easy, walkable access to the town. Discover the heritage of Bengal's Renaissance, explore the tribal origins of culture or simply relax.
